Frequently Asked Questions about Sorority Life and Recruitment

  • What is sorority recruitment?

    Sorority recruitment is the process where potential new members meet different sororities to find the one that best fits their values and goals.

  • How do I prepare for recruitment?

    Be yourself, dress appropriately for each event, and research the sororities beforehand to understand their values and activities.

  • What are the benefits of joining a sorority?

    Joining a sorority can provide lifelong friendships, networking opportunities, leadership experience, and chances to participate in philanthropic events.

  • Are there costs associated with joining?

    Yes, there are membership dues and other fees. It's important to ask about these during the recruitment process to understand the financial commitment.
